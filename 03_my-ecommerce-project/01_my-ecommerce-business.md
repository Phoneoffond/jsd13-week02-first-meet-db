# My e-Commerce business is "I need someone." 
# (Friend for Hiring Service) 
# Friends in need, Friend indeed
# Along with U

- Pain point
    - Low birth rate makes don't have their childern to take care when they want to travel or go to hospital. Sometimes, they need somebody to talk, but no one in the family is free for them.
    - Lonliness makes young generations have lots of online friends, but few friends in real life. If they want to find someone to watch a movie, have meals, or listen to their problems, there is no one beside them.
    - Parents have to work, so they cannot find time to pick-up their children home or go to school.

- Solution
    - "I need someone." (Friend for Hiring Service) makes the eldery a choice to hire someone to take care of them at the right moment they want to. If their children don't have time to be with them, they still have someone to take care or talk with them. 
    - Young generations will not feel lonely, they can have a friend in real, not the AI chatbot.
    - Parents can go to work on time, and don't have to wake up too early in the morning. Moreover, on a rainny day, you can make sure that your children can travel with someone you are trusted and monitored.

- Feature
    - You can choose staffs.
    - You can select date and time.
    - You can select price package.
    - You can monitor by live video and location.
    - You can choose vehicle; by car, bike, walk.
